Training - Unique Chart

Next, you will see charts similar to those in the previous task. However, this time one of the charts will be in a different state than all the others.

Unique chart example

In this example, the third chart is unique because it is the only chart in state 2.

Your Task

When you identify this unique chart, press the space bar. All of the charts will then be covered by a square. Use the mouse to click on the square that is covering the unique chart you just identified.

On some pages, all charts will be static, and the unique chart will exist from the beginning.

On other pages, all charts will be dynamic (moving), and there will be no unique chart at the beginning. After some time, one of the charts will transition into an unique state and remain there. This can take up to a minute.
